Metrics

NitroIQ usage and general concepts explained.

📄️ Understanding Scorecards

NitroIQ applies scores to your Sprints, Assignees, Creators, and also scores your organization on high-level concepts like Innovation Rate.

📄️ Understanding Doom Score

NitroIQ auto-scores issues in your backlog with a proprietary algorithm.

📄️ Understanding Outliers

NitroIQ naturally draws your attention to outliers in your data. Normally we will highlight Sprints or Issues that are considered to be statistical outliers.
